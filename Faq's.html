Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)

To recharge your mobile, visit our recharge page, select your operator, enter your number, choose a plan, and make payment using various secure payment options. It’s quick, easy, and secure!

We accept credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, digital wallets (e.g., Paytm, Google Pay), and other secure payment methods for a seamless transaction experience.

Log into your Mobi-Comm account, go to the "Transaction History" section, and view all your past recharges with details like date, amount, and plan.

If your recharge fails but money is deducted, don’t worry! The amount is typically refunded within 3-5 business days. Contact our support team if it takes longer.

Reach our 24/7 customer support via email (support@mobicomm.com), live chat on our website, or call our toll-free number (1-800-MOBI-COMM) listed on the "Customer Support" page.
